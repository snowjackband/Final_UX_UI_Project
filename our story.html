Our Story

Who are we?

At Nhuubala Yugal Education Centre, we are dedicated to the purpose of empowering young people to break out of the cycle of poverty.

United by our vision, we come from different backgrounds, ranging from youth work, social work, education, local business, psychology and social enterprise.

Utilising our core values of culture, community and connection, we seek to provide an innovative educational experience that speaks to the individual needs of our students.

Through all of our endeavours we hope to facilitate meaningful relationships and encourage young people to strive for the betterment of the community.

In addition to a handful of passionate and experienced ground staff, Nhuubala Yugal Education Centre is supported by six Enactus MQ members.

Passionate about the improvement of educational services, both teams are excited to see such positive student outcomes after just one and a half years.

To learn more about our unique skills and capabilities, please visit the ‘Our Team’ page.

Our Partners

Ephraim House

Paul & Erin Strahan are hometown missionaries in Moree, NSW, and with the help of volunteers, they have been facilitating numerous community service projects over the past 15 years. These include a Juvenile Justice Outreach, a weekly Night Youth Outreach, evening tutoring sessions, pick up and drop off services, and regular food runs and community dinners.

Enactus Macquarie

Enactus is a global organisation which fosters the development of social enterprises. Many universities, including 11 in Australia, have a team affiliated with Enactus. Each team aims to identify problems in different communities, before working to help develop social enterprises addressing these needs.

Hands and Feet Inc

Hands and Feet Inc is an organisation with a vision to provide sustainable help to disadvantaged people in local and international communities. This is articulated in the mission of providing food, clothing and shelter to sustain and protect those in need, providing medical help to maintain health and providing educational programmes to empower people.
